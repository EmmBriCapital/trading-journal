Upload your NinjaTrader executions export
How to export from NinjaTrader 8:
Control Center → New → Trade Performance → pick account & date range → click the Executions tab → right-click anywhere in the table → Export → save as CSV.

What EdgeLog does: NinjaTrader exports one row per fill, not per trade. EdgeLog reads your INSTRUMENT, ACTION (Buy/Sell), QUANTITY, PRICE, TIME, E/X, and COMMISSION columns and automatically pairs Entry and Exit fills into complete round-trip trades, calculating P&L using the correct point value for each futures contract.
