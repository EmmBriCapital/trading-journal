Excursion analytics
Unlock MAE / MFE insights
MAE (Maximum Adverse Excursion) and MFE (Maximum Favorable Excursion) tell you how far price moved against and in your favor before each trade closed. Together they reveal entry quality, stop placement efficiency, and how much profit you leave on the table.
1
Open any trade from the Trade Log
2
Go to the Stats tab in the trade detail panel
3
Enter the MAE Price (worst tick) and MFE Price (best tick)
4
Return here — charts populate automatically as you add more trades
